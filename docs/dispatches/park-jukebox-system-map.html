Build order

Hardest risk first. Each phase ends with a moment you can feel.

Phase 1

Sync magic

Two phones, one MP3, a manual sync slider. Then grow to four and make calibration automatic.

Done when: 4 phones play one song in perfect sync in your living room.
AVAudioEngine · clock sync
Phase 2

The party

Host or join a session. Shared queue, voting, song files flying between phones.

Done when: friends cue songs without ever touching your phone.

Phase 3

Trust

Accounts, verification, and host powers: skip, mute, remove.

Done when: only verified people can join or queue.
Sign in with Apple · CloudKit

The look
